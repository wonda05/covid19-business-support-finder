Ontario - Deferral of WSIB Premium Reporting and Payments

This advice was last updated on 04/16/20 at 8:00 PST.

WSIB Ontario is providing some relief to employers by deferring WSIB premium reporting and payments in response to COVID-19.

What it is

WSIB Ontario is offering a financial relief package to Ontario businesses to help reduce the financial burden of the COVID-19 situation. It allows businesses to defer WSIB premium reporting and payments until August 31, 2020.

Who is eligible

Ontario businesses that have the reporting and payment obligation as one of the following will be qualified for the deferral:

  • Monthly: March 31, April 30, May 31, June 30, July 31, Aug 31
  • Quarterly: April 30, July 31
  • Annual: April 30

All payment obligations (weekly and monthly) for Schedule 2 businesses will be also deferred until August 31, 2020.

How to access

No application needed. Eligible businesses are automatically receiving this financial relief package.
