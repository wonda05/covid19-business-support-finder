Business Rates Holiday (England)

This advice was last updated on 28/03/20 at 18:59 GMT.

12-month break on business rates for businesses in specific sectors across England.

What it is

Businesses in a number of eligible sectors don’t have to pay business rates for 12 months, covering the 2020/21 tax period. This will support them as they work to maintain property payments and cover ongoing costs.

Who is eligible

Businesses in specific sectors: shops, restaurants, cafes, drinking establishments, cinemas, live music venues, estate agents, letting agencies, bingo halls, leisure establishments, assembly halls, hotels, guest/boarding premises, self-catering accommodation and nurseries.

How to access

No action should be required as these changes should be made automatically for your next business rates tax bill for April 2020.​ However, in some cases local authorities may have to reissue your bill automatically to exclude the business rate charge​.
