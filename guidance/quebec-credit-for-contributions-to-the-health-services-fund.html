Québec – Credit for contributions to the Health Services Fund

This advice was last updated on 05/05/20 at 10:10 PDT.

Credit for contributions to the Health Services Fund for Québec businesses that have employees on paid leave due to COVID-19.

What it is

Employers that are eligible for the Canada Emergency Wage Subsidy and that have an establishment in Québec can apply for a credit for contributions to the health services fund for employees on paid leave due to the COVID-19 pandemic for a period of twelve weeks, retroactive to March 15, 2020.

Who is eligible

You are eligible if you are an employer that:

  • is eligible for the Canada Emergency Wage Subsidy and that has an establishment in Québec​
  • has employees on paid leave for a week or more for the period starting March 15, 2020 and ending June 6, 2020

How to access

The application for the credit for contributions to the health services fund must be filed with Revenu Québec at the same time as the Summary of Source Deductions and Employer Contributions (RLZ-1.S-V or RLZ-1.ST-V) for 2020.
