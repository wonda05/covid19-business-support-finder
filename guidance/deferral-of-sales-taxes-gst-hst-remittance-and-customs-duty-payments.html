Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) – Deferral of Sales Tax (GST/HST) Remittance and Customs Duty Payments​

This advice was last updated on 04/07/20 at 22:35 PDT.

As a result of the COVID-19 pandemic, the CRA has provided some relief to businesses, including self-employed individuals, to defer GST/HST remittance or customs duty payments owing on their imports.

What it is

The CRA has deferred GST/HST remittances and customs duty payments to allow organizations more time to make payments.

Who is eligible

You are an eligible if you:

  • Are a:
    • Self-employed individual
    • Business
  • Have a GST/HST remittances owing from March 27, 2020 to May 31, 2020

or

  • Have customs duty payments on imports owing from March 27, 2020 to May 31, 2020

How to access

No application needed.
