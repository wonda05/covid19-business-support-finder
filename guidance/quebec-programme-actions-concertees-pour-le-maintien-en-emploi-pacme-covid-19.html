Québec - Programme actions concertées pour le maintien en emploi (PACME–COVID-19)

This advice was last updated on 04/16/20 at 16:00 PDT.

The Québec government is offering direct financial support to businesses to promote workforce skills training and human resource management.

What it is

Effective April 6, 2020 and retroactive to March 15, 2020, the Québec government will reimburse employers for eligible training expenses (within limits) where their normal operations have been disrupted by COVID-19.

Who is eligible

You are eligible if you are an employer with employees in Québec and your business has been affected by COVID-19.

How to access

To apply to the PACME, contact an Entreprises Québec business adviser in your region.

Where to get more information
