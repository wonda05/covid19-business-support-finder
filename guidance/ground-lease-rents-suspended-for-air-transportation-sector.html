Ground Lease Rents Suspended for Air Transportation Sector

This advice was last updated on 04/08/20 at 14:19 PST.

A support measure for airport authorities by waiving ground lease rents through end of 2020.

What it is

The Government of Canada is providing relief to the air transportation sector to help airports reduce cost pressure by waiving the rent payments from March 2020 through December 2020.

Who is eligible

You are eligible:

  • If you are:
    • one of the 21 National Airport System airport authorities
    • PortsToronto, which operates the Billy Bishop Toronto City Airport

How to access

No application needed.
