£25,000 Retail and Hospitality Grant Scheme (Northern Ireland)

This advice was last updated on 28/03/20 at 18:57 GMT.

Cash payments for small businesses in specific sectors across Northern Ireland.

What it is

A scheme offering a one-off £25,000 grant to eligible retail, hospitality and leisure businesses.

Who is eligible

Businesses with a rateable value of up to £51,000. Click here for more details.

How to access

Local authorities are expected to make these payments automatically. If you’re concerned they may not have your correct contact or bank details, please contact them directly.
