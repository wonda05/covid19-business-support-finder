Coronavirus business interruption loan scheme

Help for businesses struggling with cashflow due to the coronavirus

What is it

A loan scheme for small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) that are struggling with cash flow because of revenues that have been deferred or lost due to the coronavirus outbreak. Loans are being offered on generous terms to support SMEs.

Who is eligible

Any UK-based business that has a turnover less than £45m is eligible for the Coronavirus Business Interruption Loan Scheme.
