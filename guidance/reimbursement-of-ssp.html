Reimbursement of Statutory Sick Pay

This advice was last updated on 28/03/20 at 18:27 GMT.

Reimbursing businesses for SSP payments made in relation to the coronavirus.

What it is

Government will reimburse businesses for SSP where employees are absent due to coronavirus. SSP is paid from day 1 of absence up to 2 weeks.

Who is eligible

Businesses with fewer than 250 employees.

How to access

Whilst the scheme is effective immediately, HMRC are working hard to setup the payment mechanism. As soon as this is announced, we’ll update our guidance to help you access this.
